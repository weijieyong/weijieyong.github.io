1. Organize thoughts and learn at the same time

I felt like much of my time is spent mindlessly scrolling through YouTube to find the next video to watch. I find it often that I just consume most of the content without taking in something useful. I want to spend my time better. Sitting down and writing will force me to think and organize my thoughts and learn at the same time. Writing a blog forces me to refine my idea, thoughts for the reader, and future-me. I will also need to research and understand a topic well to be able to explain and blog about it.

“If you can't explain it to a six year old, you don't understand it yourself.”

- Albert Einstein

2. Being able to express myself better

Another reason for me to start this blog is for me to learn to express myself better.
It's not easy converting thoughts into words. Unless we are able to communicate by just our thoughts, I think being able to make yourself clear in words is very important. I'm quite an introvert when being with strangers. I find it hard to speak out or allow my thoughts and ideas to be heard. But I have a lot of ideas, mostly weird and quirky ones, but I do believe there's someone who is cool out there who will appreciate it. Maybe one day meet you in person. I hope this blog could be an outlet for my creative juice to flow.

3. Document my journey

One of the reasons for me to start this blog is for me to document my journey and thoughts at the moment, for future me to look back and see how far I've come. Of course, it would be a bonus if my blog is able to help or bring joy to anyone out there.

"Document. Don’t create."

- Gary Vaynerchuck

GOALS

My goal for this blog is to publish 1 article per week. I won't be focusing on metrics or how to monetize this blog at first. I tried doing that before, it does more harm than good. I believe that focusing on writing about things that I'm interested in and finding fulfillment in it helps me to be able to stick with it long term.

Create Like Nobody's Watching

Topics that I'm interested in:

Technology, Money, Engineering & Science, Entrepreneurship, Self-Improving, Foods, Travelling
